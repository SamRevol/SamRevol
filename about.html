Howdy!

Nice to meet you. I'm an art director raised in Milwaukee USA, living in France between Paris and Provence with my husband Ben and a handful of plants.

I am bilingual in English and French and have worked and studied in both languages. Just don't ask me to pronounce écureuil or grenouille...

My passion for art began as early as I can remember. One of my first memories is sitting on my kitchen floor, paintbrush in hand, American girl doll to my right; working on a mini masterpiece on a doll-sized easel. I am ever recognizant of my parents' role in fostering my creativity throughout my childhood and encouraging me to pursue art as a career path. Over the years I graduated to people sized easels, and that doll has been replaced by a big cup of coffee and occasionally my mother-in-law's cat, Mimi.

Aside from visual art making, I host La Vie en Bros, a podcast about finding humor in the international experience by bringing on guests to discuss Parisian life and more broadly French culture.

In my free time I love to bake, run, thrift, visit museums and host events. I'm lucky to have a small network of Midwesterners in Paris, and we love to host cultural celebrations and create new traditions year-round.
