2022 New Year Greeting Card

Project at L'Institut Supérieur des Arts Appliqués

This was my first project using Adobe AfterEffects. I had one week to learn the software and to make a minute-long greeting card. I used my existing tarot card illustrations from a coloring book I designed.

Partnership with Musée de la Chasse et de la Nature: Group work

Art Showcase: Le Diorama Anthropocène by François Malingrëy and RIP Passenger Pigeons by Brandon Balengée

My group-mates and I were charged with the task of creating two, one minute videos showcasing two works at the Museum of Hunting and Nature in Paris while respecting their brand design. We decided to highlight the depth of the first artist's paintings by animating them with a parallax effect. Both works reference the negative impact of man on nature, so we chose a serious tone to ensure respect to the art.
